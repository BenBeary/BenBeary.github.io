Ben Beary

Last Updated 4/7/2023

Ben is a 21-year-old male studying at San Jose State University for a BFA in Digital Media Arts. Ever since high school, they have dreamed for a career in the games industry. During his time in high school, he learned multiple coding languages such as Javascript, C#, C++ while also learning art programs such as Photoshop, Illustrator, and Maya.

After finishing high school at the start of the 2020 pandemic, they moved on towards attending a community college where he would take the next two years earning transfer credits for his GE and then transfer to a college. During this time his knack for game design fell under as his motivation plummeted during the pandemic. Ben still worked on improving their art skills, but not with the same vigor he had back in high school. After finishing his GE courses during the spring of 2022, Ben transferred to San Jose State and was able to gain some motivation to start working on game design once more. They joined a Game Design Club that was on campus and has been making games with other people. Since then, he has been a part of the making of three different games with one being their very own side project.
