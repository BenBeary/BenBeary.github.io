ART 12 | Paper Crafts

For this assignment we had to trace an image down to four colors in black and white. There was a second portion of the assignment that involved scrapping magazines for four different colors that follow the same color guide as the black and white portion.

The cutting portion was done with an exacto knife. The process was tedious and slow depending on the amount of detail being cut out.
