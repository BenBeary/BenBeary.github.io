Game Lab 1

Created: 9/7/23

Tags:

ART 108 | Games | Lab Word Count: 112

The First game we ended up playing was Cheat or also known as Bullshit. We played a couple of rounds before actually logging any results so people unfamilar, such as myself, could learn and figure out how the game is played.

Picture From Cheat

Picture From Go Fish!

We didn't want to think too hard for another game that could support 5 people, so we went with Go Fish! for our second game. There was a little confusion while playing as most people were only used to playing the game with 2 people, but everyone figured it out in the end and the game turned out quite fun with everyone getting at least one set of pairs.
