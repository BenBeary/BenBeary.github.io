Shapes Design

Created: 10/23/2022

Tags:

Adobe Illustrator | Art 74

For this assignment, we used nine different letters to create unique shapes out of using high contrasting shots. I mainly used the invert tool to cause colors to be inverted if overlapping with another letter. It was really fun creating complex shapes and found the S shape to look the best.
