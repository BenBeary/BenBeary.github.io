Portrait Animation

Created: 12/10/2022

Tags:

Adobe Animate | Illustrator | ART 74

This was probably the most fun project in the school year as it let the student have creative freedom for how the video would look like. The only requirements were a theme, vector style image of a head, and tween animations. I thought my head cut-out turned out awesome. I had some trouble with adobe animate as it had been a while since I last used it. After a short break away from it, I was able to come back and figure out how to properly tween and create the video really quickly.
