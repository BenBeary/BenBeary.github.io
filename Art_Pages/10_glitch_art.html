Glitch Art

Created: 10/20/2022

Tags:

PhotoShop | Audacity | ART 74

This Project's goal was to explore the process of how image data is stored and can be manipulated. I ended up having trouble manipulating the images as Windows detects manipulated images and blocks it from being shown. Audacity was, however, able to get past this issue and glitch the images. We then edited the distorted images in Photoshop.
