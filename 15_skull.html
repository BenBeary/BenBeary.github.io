ART 24 | Charcoal

This was our first assignment that involved working in high detail with charcoal. We used images of skulls as references and had to try our best to upscale the image to a two foot big sheet of paper. We also used a different drawing method where we first blacked the canvas in charcoal and erased the charcoal to define the skull.
