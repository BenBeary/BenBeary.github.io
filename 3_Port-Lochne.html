I did not make any of the prefabs or textures

This is my first official map ever made and was made entirely from scratch. I used references for the buildings and created out the map layout on my own. The main concept for the map was a seaport with a train depot above the seaport

full view of the map

Viewport from the engine

One of the main problems I faced was that the map was too big. To limit the size I created natural bariers with invisible barriers to make sure no one could go out of bounds
